TIC is a Design, Project Management and Turnkey Fit Out Contracting Company. Our success has been translated in our commitment to combine our client's vision with our collective skills and experience in a mutual quest to achieve each project’s full potential. Understanding design & detail with a defined beginning & end result are the key considerations, irrespective of size or scale of project. Planning, Organizing and then Management of the resources, results in successful completion & client satisfaction.

The Interior Consortium is a team of professionals with hands on experience in retail with reputed groups like Landmark, Arvind Lifestyle Brands Limited, Avenue, Blossom trading etc. We understand that the retail designs’ primary goal is to make it commercially viable, while aesthetics of the store shall be in line with customer profile. TIC is instrumental in developing innovative concepts while keeping all the practical working aspects.

The design team is led by Mr. Shirish Jain, architect by profession. As a part of landmark group for over 9 years, Shirish was part of the organic growth that retail conglomerate achieved in the Middle East, Europe & India. Shirish headed various concept & project teams with a 360° view of retail, projects and business. TIC, with its retail competency works with retailers across the Middle East, Africa & India.
